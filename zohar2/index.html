Academic Pathways

Nurturing Every Stage

01

Foundation Phase

Grade R - Grade 3

We believe in the power of play and curiosity. Our foundation phase builds the crucial cornerstones of literacy and numeracy while planting the first seeds of faith.

  • Play-based Learning
  • Bible Stories
  • Phonetics
  • Motor Skills
02

Intermediate Phase

Grade 4 - Grade 6

Transitioning from concrete to abstract thinking. Students begin to explore specialized subjects, discovering their unique God-given talents in science, arts, and languages.

  • Natural Sciences
  • Coding & Robotics
  • Public Speaking
  • Team Sports
03

Senior Phase

Grade 7 - Grade 10

Rigorous preparation for the Cambridge IGCSEs. We focus on critical analysis, global perspectives, and leadership development to prepare them for the world.

  • Cambridge IGCSE
  • Leadership
  • Career Guidance
  • Community Service
